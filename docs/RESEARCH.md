## Key takeaways applied to MEGATACH

1. **CDO, not RCDSO** — College of Denturists of Ontario regulates denturists. RCDSO is for dentists. (We had this wrong initially.)
2. **CDCP Provider badge is critical** — Canadian Dental Care Plan rolled out 2024, every major Ontario denturist site now displays it.
3. **"In-house lab" is the differentiation** — Dentures Direct proves it converts. We lead with this in the trust strip.
4. **Free consultation + 24-hour response promise** are table-stakes — we added both to the contact page.
5. **Almost every Ontario competitor uses navy + white** — opportunity to differentiate, but we matched the supplied brand's teal accent (`#29B1C5`) to honour the existing logo.
6. **Service slate** includes Immediate Dentures (post-extraction same-day) — common across all winners; added as a 5th service.
7. **Trust signals** to maintain over time: CDO registration, DAO/DAC membership, CDCP provider, implant brand partnerships (for implant page), AODA statement.

## Pricing transparency note

Most Ontario denturist clinics do NOT publish prices, but a growing minority publish ranges in cost-guide blog posts (Apple Denture, Ottawa South Denture). Worth considering for SEO once the site has organic traffic.

Ontario implant-supported denture benchmark: **$4,000–$6,000 per arch** starting; **$2,500–$5,000 per implant**.

## Photography note

Stock dental imagery is the single biggest credibility weakness for a denture clinic site. Grand Street, Aventura, and Dentures Direct all win on original lifestyle + lab photography. Initial scaffold uses Unsplash stock; replace with original lab + patient photography ASAP for production launch.
